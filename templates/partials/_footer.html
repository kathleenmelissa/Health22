The Wellbeing Heart Hospital is a private, not-for-profit institution committed to making high calibre healthcare and medical education accessible to all. Our main hospital campus is located in the Parklands area of Nairobi. In addition, we have 50 Medical and Diagnostic Centres ​ throughout East Africa providing primary and specialised care as well as various diagnostic tests. Our Patient Welfare Programme also offers financial assistance to uninsured and low-income patients who would otherwise be unable to afford the medical care they require.​
